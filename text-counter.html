About This Tool

Our Text Counter tool helps you analyze your text content with detailed statistics including:

  • Word Count: Number of words in your text
  • Character Count: Total characters with and without spaces
  • Sentence Count: Number of sentences based on punctuation
  • Paragraph Count: Number of paragraphs separated by blank lines
  • Reading Time: Estimated time to read the text (200 words per minute)
  • Speaking Time: Estimated time to speak the text (150 words per minute)

Use Cases

  • Academic writing: Meet word count requirements
  • Content writing: Optimize article length for SEO
  • Social media: Ensure posts meet character limits
  • Copywriting: Analyze text for marketing effectiveness
  • Legal documents: Track document length and complexity
